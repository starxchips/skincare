✨  Golden Rules
💜
Copper Peptides = Recovery nights only
Never use Copper Peptides on the same night as retinoid or acids. They belong on Tue, Thu, Sat — the nights your skin is resting between actives.
🚫
Retinoid + Acid = never same night
Good Genes (lactic acid) and Retinoid should never share an evening. Always separated by a recovery night in between.
☀️
SPF every single morning
Even indoors. Even cloudy. Even for 5 minutes outside. Retinoid and acids make your skin photosensitive — skipping SPF undoes everything.
↑
Always upward strokes
Every product, every time. Massage upward, never downward. Gravity already does the work — don't help it along.
💧
Hyaluronic Acid = damp skin, always
Dry skin + HA = pulls moisture OUT of deeper layers. Always mist slightly before applying. This is the #1 most common application mistake.
🌙
First month: 2x retinoid per week max
Start at Mon + Fri only for the first 3–4 weeks. If no irritation, you're already on the right schedule. Never rush retinoid frequency.
